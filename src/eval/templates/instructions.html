[These instructions are repeated on subsequent pages as well.]


In this task, you will be presented with {{ evals_per_worker }} partial dialogues, one after the other. For each dialogue, you will be given a list of candidate responses that could come next in the dialogue. For each sentence, please rate whether it is a sensible next sentence or not, given the context, by rating the sentence as -1 (not sensible), 1 (sensible), or 0 (ambiguous or unclear). Your goal is to identify sentences that would lead to a coherent dialogue in English.


For each dialogue, you will also be presented with a "context" that the dialogue was performed in. The dialogues in these tasks are negotiations between buyers and sellers based on Craigslist posts, and so the context for each dialogue is the Craigslist post that the negotiation was based on. You will also be given the role (buyer or seller) of each person in the dialogue.
